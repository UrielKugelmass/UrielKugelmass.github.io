6. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?
My tip of the week is an app called f.lux, which I have been using for years now. It simply manages the dominant colors in the screen (macOS, no idea if this app exists for any other OS) and I have it set up so that after 10 pm it turns yellowish, so that I don’t have bright colors on my face right before bed. I think it is important to take care of our eyes’ health, especially when we are constantly in front of our laptops in this field.
