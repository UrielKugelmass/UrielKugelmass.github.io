5. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?
My tip of the week is not a tool or framework. It is more of a concept that I have seen in the workplace but unfortunately I never had a professor that mentioned it in a class. “Impostor syndrome” happens a lot in the field of Software Engineering, and it is the feeling when one believes he/she is not qualified for his/her job. It can have serious consequences, and today I encountered an article discussing it here. I think it is very important that we all know about such a concept as it may affect us in the near future, or even our colleagues and friends.
