5. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?
My tip of the week is a plugin called Cageify. Last Summer, during my internship, it was fairly common to "prank" people if they left their cubicles for a couple minutes to go to the restroom for example. On my first week, I made the mistake of leaving my laptop unlocked and it took me some days to realize that Chrome was replacing all pictures on the browser with images of Nicolas Cage. The tip here is not so much to make people upset but rather to make sure we lock our machines whether at work or at school or anywhere we are to keep our work and lives private in case someone takes our stuff.
