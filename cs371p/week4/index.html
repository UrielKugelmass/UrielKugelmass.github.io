5. What's my pick-of-the-week or tip-of-the-week?
My tip of the week is a plugin called Noisli. Sometimes it happens to me that I am working in the GDC or in a public space, and some people are having conversations close to me. This makes it really hard for me to focus on my work so I need a way to isolate my mind from what's going on next to me. I used to plug in my headphones and play music, but the songs' lyrics were also a distraction. But then I tried Noisli, which is a tool that lets you build your own "combos". These are combinations of natural sounds (rain, fire, thunderstorms, etc) that can be relaxing and help me be more productive.
